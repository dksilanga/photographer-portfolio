Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my photography services

How far in advance should I book my session?

+

It's best to book at least 2-3 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 6-12 months in advance for weddings to ensure availability.

What should I wear for my photo session?

+

I recommend solid colors and avoiding busy patterns. For group photos, coordinating colors work best. I provide a detailed style guide after booking.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

+

Portrait sessions typically take 1-2 weeks for delivery. Weddings and events take 4-6 weeks. Rush processing is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer prints or just digital files?

+

I offer both! You'll receive high-resolution digital files with print release, and I also offer professional printing services through my lab partners.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

I understand that life happens. Sessions can be rescheduled with at least 48 hours notice. Wedding deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a new date if available.
